Our Practice

Local healthcare with long-term relationships

Coastal Medical Rooms has been part of the Kāpiti community for more than two decades. Our long-standing team of doctors and nurses provides family-centred care built on trust, familiarity and genuine relationships.

We pair the warmth of a local practice with modern healthcare tools — an online patient portal, same-day acute appointments, and a dedicated Health Improvement Practitioner — so looking after your health feels simple and personal.
